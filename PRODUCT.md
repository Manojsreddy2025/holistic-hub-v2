# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Patients and their families in India and abroad — all consultations happen online over secure video — most of them managing chronic physical conditions or seeking psychological counseling, often after conventional treatment has disappointed them. Many are older or browsing on a phone handed to them by a relative. They arrive anxious and skeptical; the site's job is to make them feel calm, respected, and safe enough to request an appointment. Privacy rule: the site publishes no personal contact details — no phone number, no WhatsApp, no personal email.

## Product Purpose

The marketing site for Holistic Heal Hub, the practice of Dr. Sammeta Madhavi — Homeopathic Physician & Psychotherapy Counselor (Reg. No. A 6966, 25+ years, BHMS Homoeopathy + MS Psychotherapy + Bio-Informatics). It presents the practice's two care arms (Physical Treatment and Psychological Treatment), establishes Dr. Madhavi's credentials, and converts visits into consultation bookings. Success is a patient requesting an online appointment through the site.

## Positioning

One physician treats the body and the mind together — homoeopathy and psychotherapy in the same room, so patients are treated as whole people, not as a list of symptoms.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Request an online consultation (appointment form).
- Secondary CTA: email the practice at DrMadhavi@holistichealhub.org.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "Care that treats the whole of you."
- Belief ladder: (1) this practice is real and credentialed → (2) treating body and mind together is why other treatments failed me → (3) Dr. Madhavi has done this for 25+ years and is trusted internationally → (4) booking is one tap away.
- Proof on hand: Reg. No. A 6966; BHMS + MS Psychotherapy + Bio-Informatics; 25+ years in practice; 2024 collaboration with Cochies Oncology Centre, USA (Dr. Nettleton) running homoeopathy alongside radiation/chemo for side-effect relief; the practice's own motto "Happiness is a journey, not a destination."

## Brand Personality

Calm, credentialed, humane. The feel of a calm, light-filled apothecary: unhurried, human, trustworthy. The site should lower the visitor's pulse, not raise it. Warmth carried by typography, botanical motifs, and one decisive natural photograph — never by hype or urgency.

## Anti-references

- Stock smiling-doctor-with-stethoscope photography and hospital-blue corporate medical templates.
- The AI landing-page scaffold: hero → three icon cards → CTA, tracked uppercase eyebrows over every section, big-number stat rows.
- Wellness-influencer pastel gradients and vague "healing energy" copy with no credentials behind it.

## Design Principles

- Calm is the conversion strategy: every choice should make an anxious visitor feel safer.
- Credentials over claims: show the registration number, degrees, and the Cochies collaboration; never puff.
- The whole person is the story: body and mind are always presented as two halves of one care philosophy.
- One decisive image beats five generic ones.
- Editorial structure over card grids: sections read like a well-set book, not a SaaS template.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Older patients on mobile are a primary audience: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, generous type sizes, large touch targets, easy access to the appointment request from every page, full reduced-motion alternatives.
